Prix Cime Resdiency Award

26/09/2025

I am extremly happy to have been awarded the 'Residency Prix CIME' as parte of the Prix CIME 2025 Electroacoustic Music Composition. My work 'Travelling without Moving' was selected as one of eight works among 175 compositions for a residency in renowned research centres such as Influx - Musiques and Reserches in Belgium, HDK in Zurich, Tempo Reale in Firenze and more.

The competition received 597 anonymous submissions from all around the world.

422 (70.7%) submissions in Section 1: Prix CIME – Open to all composers regardless of age

175 (29.3%) submissions in Section 2: Residency Prix CIME – Open to all composers born after 12/31/1990.

72.7% stereo, 13.1% octophonic, 6.9% ambisonics, and 7.3% other.

More info on the website: https://www.cime-icem.net
